# Investigation of Data Loss Prevention (DLP) Policy Violation

## Scenario
A DLP alert was generated indicating a potential data policy violation involving sensitive information.  
The alert required investigation to determine whether the activity was legitimate or a policy breach.

## Tools Used
- Symantec DLP
- Microsoft Purview DLP
- Endpoint monitoring tools

## Investigation
- Reviewed DLP alert details, including the triggered policy, type of sensitive data, and user activity.  
- Confirmed that the activity involved sensitive data but was initiated for a legitimate business purpose.  
- No signs of malicious intent, repeated policy violations, or attempts to bypass security controls were observed.

## Response & Remediation
- Documented the incident according to standard DLP procedures.  
- Educated the user on proper handling of sensitive data to prevent future policy violations.  
- Closed the alert after verification that no further violations occurred.

## Outcome
- Risk of data exposure mitigated.  
- No further DLP alerts generated for the user.  
- Security awareness improved for the individual involved.

## Learning
- Gained experience in evaluating DLP alerts and understanding user behavior.  
- Learned to balance security enforcement with legitimate business operations.  
- Improved skills in documenting incidents and following organizational procedures.
